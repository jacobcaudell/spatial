St. Ignatius Statue: 3D Line Drawing Splats

Gasson Hall St. Ignatius Statue

This statue of St. Ignatius Loyola stands on the Boston College campus. The 3D visualization below showcases a hybrid contour style with watercolor elements, created using Amrit Kwatra's innovative approach to 3D Gaussian Splatting. His method transforms drone-captured photographs into informative line drawings that preserve geometry and semantics, then trains 3D Gaussian Splats on these stylized images to create interactive 3D line drawings.
